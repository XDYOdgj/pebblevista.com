Sunew Bamboo Socks – Perfect for Active Lifestyles

Designed for those who lead an active life, these unisex bamboo socks combine comfort, durability, and eco-friendliness into one essential pair. Ideal for hiking, workouts, or daily wear.

Soft, Breathable & Supportive

Made from 80% bamboo viscose, these socks are incredibly soft and gentle on your skin while offering superior breathability. The lightweight fabric keeps your feet cool and wicks away moisture, keeping you comfortable all day long.

  • Ultra-soft bamboo viscose fabric
  • Natural wicking properties for moisture control
  • Snug fit with elastic blend prevents slipping
  • Perfect for workouts, hiking, and everyday use
  • Available in small, medium, large, and X-large sizes
  • Machine washable for easy maintenance

Y-Shaped Heel Design

The unique Y-shaped heel design ensures the socks stay securely in place, so you don’t have to worry about them slipping off during your most intense workouts or long outdoor adventures.

Durable & Flexible

With a blend of 15% polyester and 5% elastic, these socks are built to last while maintaining flexibility. Whether you're on the move or at rest, they support your feet without compromising on comfort or style.

Perfect for Every Activity

These Sunew Bamboo Socks are your go-to choice for any activity—whether you're hitting the gym, trekking through the mountains, or running errands. They're ideal for both men and women, making them a versatile addition to your wardrobe.

Great Value with Three Pairs Included

Each pack includes three pairs of these high-quality bamboo socks, so you’ll always have a fresh pair ready for every adventure. A perfect blend of sustainability, comfort, and performance for all your outdoor and indoor needs.
